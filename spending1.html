Spend towards freedom

Spending for Show vs. Financial Wellness

“Spending money to show people how much money you have is the fastest way to have less money”

The Psychology of Money by Morgan Housel.

One of the main reasons why people don't feel well with their personal finances is because of this believe.

For example, do you buy expensive clothes just to show others that you can afford them? Then this kind of habit makes you have less money over and over.

The same can be applied to cars, housing and many different things.

  • You can still dress well within budget.
  • You can go to point A to point B without owning an expensive car.
  • You can text, make phone calls and surf the Internet without the trendiest phone.

Think about it, you are wasting resources to impress people that are not going to help you pay your bills at the end of the month.
